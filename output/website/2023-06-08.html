Local COVID-19 transmission

For statistics on locally acquired COVID-19 cases and tests in past 7 days and past four weeks by local health district or Local Government Area, please visit Locally acquired COVID-19 cases and tests.

NSW respiratory surveillance reports - COVID-19 and influenza

Public health experts carefully review case information and monitor local testing rates to understand the incidence, severity, and distribution of COVID-19 and influenza in NSW.

View all NSW respiratory surveillance reports or further information about Influenza notifications in NSW residents.

NSW sewage surveillance

The NSW Sewage Surveillance Program samples 4 sites in Greater Sydney and Newcastle on a weekly basis as sentinel sites and to monitor COVID variants. Results are published in the weekly NSW respiratory surveillance report.

COVID-19 vaccination

For the latest COVID-19 vaccination data in Australia and NSW, visit Department of Health - Vaccination numbers and statistics.

Notes

This week includes data for the 7 days ending 4pm 08 June 2023. Last week includes data to the week ending 4pm 01 June 2023. Numbers of cases are based on date of notification to NSW Health. COVID-19 related deaths are notified to NSW Health from a range of sources and not all deaths reported by NSW Health occurred in the week in which they are reported, as there is sometimes a delay between a death occurring and it being notified to NSW Health.
